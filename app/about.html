Thanks for taking a look!

This app is inspired by the concept of genetic algorithms, which attempt to generate solutions to optimization problems. Here, the problem is getting as close as possible to a target color. While this is hardly difficult problem (heck, we know the RGB values!), the app serves more as a simulation of genetic algorithms themselves and can be used as a teaching tool.
